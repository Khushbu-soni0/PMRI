Our Philosophy

We recognize that science is the foundation upon which successful drug development is built. Our team of experts delivering the highest quality is our mission. "It is very easy to say that quality is at the core of our philosophy; however, it is an entirely different story to actually live up to this ideal. Deeply engrained into our culture it is the differentiating factor that helps PMRI reach client objectives and maintain the highest level of success. True customer focus. Communication is key! Our Impact

Together with our valued sponsors we play an integral role in getting new life saving drugs, drugs that are repurposed, biosimilars and affordable medicines to those in need. "We strive to reach the common goal of improving access to safer, effective medicines for countries around the world by conducting scientifically sound studies with exceptional quality. Having conducted over 5000 clinical trials every year we offer our clients the expertise of moving a drug from its conception to market approval. We pride ourselves on our proven quality, scientific integrity, flexibility, rapid turnaround and a unique customer-focused approach.
