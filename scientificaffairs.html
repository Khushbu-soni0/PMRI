Scientific Affairs

We recognize that science is the foundation upon which successful drug development is built. Our Scientific Affairs team plays a pivotal role in contributing to overseeing the integrity of the study process. With our expansive library of in-house data, our industry expertise and our experience working with global regulatory agencies, we develop study designs and project plans for regulatory markets around the world. We offer comprehensive support services to help navigate from pre-clinical to regulatory submission.
