Our highly skilled Biostatisticians and Clinical SAS Programmers have a wealth of experience in working with the various regulatory requirements. With this experience and knowledge we can offer you a full range of biostatistical and SAS programming services to suit your needs, including but not limited to, CDISC services, Table, Listing, and Figure (TLFs), and statistical analysis. Clinical Data Management

From study start-up to database lock, our onsite team of experienced CDM Project Managers and Data Entry personnel are dedicated to providing high-quality deliverables as part of our comprehensive package of CDM services. Our team has extensive experience across multiple therapeutic areas. We are a gold member of the Clinical Data Interchange Standards Consortium (CDISC). Our deliverables follow CDISC and Clinical Data Acquisition Standards Harmonization (CDASH) standards and this will allow you to accelerate your regulatory submission and review process. Whether you require a full CDM package or only data entry services, we will proactively work with you to accommodate your requirements while maintaining the integrity of our proven CDM processes. We use TrialMaster, a validated US FDA 21 CFR Part 11 compliant Electronic Data Capture (EDC) suite which is the most intuitive EDC suite on the market. Our vast library of electronic Case Report Form (eCRF) templates, allow for the rapid build and deployment of clinical study databases, and no down time during mid-study changes.
