Available Clinical Services

You can always count on PMRI when you need us. Our GCP-compliant custom built clinical unit, with over 115,000 square foot of space, provides sufficient capacity to meet all of your needs, even during the COVID period. Boasting 360 beds in 6 self-contained state-of-the art units Our flexible scheduling process also provides you with dosing dates right within your target range and accommodate delays and last minute requests. This is a key success factor that allows you to minimize the impact of unavoidable delays during the drug development process

Expertise

We have hands on experience with various dosage forms in single dose and multiple dose drug administration regimens with all possible designs (crossover, partial replicate, semi-replicate, full replicate). Bullet point for:Fasting, fed (high-fat, low-fat), sprinkle. Bullet point: Studies with intensive sampling, long washout. Bullet point: Special populations Bullet point: Multiple dose studies with consecutive dosing up to 15 days. Bullet point: First in human studies Bullet point: Proof of concept studies. Bullet point: Topical formluations, transdermal patches - Adhesion, Sensitization, Irritation.

Subject Selection

We have a dedicated call center and recruiters. From a large database of healthy volunteers to special populations such as post-menopausal women, we’ve got you covered! Being located in the Greater Toronto Area, gives us access to a diverse population which is what Regulators look for in clinical trials. We draw from our active database which boasts over X volunteers in addition to reaching out by various advertising medians. We also have a very effective referral program - participants love the safe and comfortable environment offered at PMRI and spread the word.

Pharmacy

Right drug, right participant. It happens here! In our secure and licensed pharmacy the critical step of repackaging for clinical trials is conducted by our trained and qualified staff. The facility is designed to store drugs under various storage conditions depending the requirements of temperature and humidity. Narcotic, controlled drug substances, including cannabis is covered with our active controlled substance licenses. Formalized procedures for blinding/unblinding, appropriate drug preparation environment and qualified staff is our solution.

Our Kitchen

“This may seems like a “”nice have”” but having full control of food content for consistency, quality and adhering to dietary requirements not only decreases study variability but improves probability of study success.” Perfect solution for food-effect studies including FDA’s critical high-fat content breakfast/meal.Our on-site kitchen works closely with our nutritionist/dietician ensuring compliance with special dietary requirements & special caloric intake and provides the highest quality and consistent portions from subject to subject and period to period. Using the freshest ingredients we create menus that consider nutrition, taste and presentation which assist with subject compliance.Our kitchen is routinely inspected by Toronto Public Health.
