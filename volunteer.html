Who We Are?

We are a Contract Research Organization (CRO) and work with clinical trial Sponsors from around the world. We have been operating in Scarborough since 1997 and are established within our community. Our focus is your health and safety, and our highly trained and qualified staff will be with you every step of the way. Our state-of-the-art facility offers a comfortable environment with great amenities. Check us out.

Refer A Friend

Our Refer-A-Friend program let's you earn money for every friend who completes a study. Spread the word and give it a try today.

Privacy

We use several safeguards to protect your personal information, such as physical (limited access areas and locked offices, cabinets and drawers), technological (computer passwords and firewalls) and organizational controls (limiting access on a "need-to-know" basis and staff training).We also have a dedicated Privacy Officer that will address any of your privacy concerns.
