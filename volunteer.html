HOW IT WORKS?

Clinical trials are in general medical or health related research studies that are conducted in human. These trials follow a defined protocol aimed to evaluate medical, surgical, or behavioral intervention to help find out if a new treatment is safe and effective and/or has less harmful side effects than the standard treatment.

Get pre-screened by phone

Joining a study starts with a quick phone call with us. We’ll ask you a few questions, and if there’s a study that might be a good fit, we’ll move into the next phase

Take a Health Assesment

After you’ve been pre-screened, you'll come to our research facuility for a complimentary screening, and to discuss any quesitons you might have

Participate

Once you're approved as a candidate and if you’re happy with the study conditions, your participation in the clinical research study will begin.

Help Advanced Medicine

When you participate in a clinical study, you help make medical advances possible, giving hope to countless people all over the world.

Who we are

We are a Contract Research Organization (CRO) and work with clinical trial Sponsors from around the world. We have been operating in Scarborough since 1997 and are established within our community. Our focus is your health and safety, and our highly trained and qualified staff will be with you every step of the way. Our state-of-the-art facility offers a comfortable environment with great amenities.
